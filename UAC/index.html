About

The Union Aerospace Corporation is a multiplanetary megacorporation that operates scientific and military facilities across the solar system. Most notablely, a large facility comparative to a small city on Mars. The UAC conducts research on teleportation technology, taking advantage of a recently disocovered martian resource called "Argent plasma". UAC is heavily invested in developing new technology, with ongoing research projects into genetic modificaton and modern weaponry.

The UAC was founded in 2015 by Thomas Kelliher. In 2095, the UAC conducted a geological survey of Mars, where they first discovered "Argent Plasma" and soon set up extraction plants to harvest the resource and generate "Argent Energy". This quickly became the Earths main power supply.

The UAC has gone through many variations of it's logo (as pictured above).

The UAC has been instrumental to finding ancient artifacts of past lifeforms on Mars. However, the exact nature of these artifacts is strictly classified to the general public.

Locations



Earth


UAC has multiple facilities located on Earth, many of which are freight hubs for transit to Mars.

UAC has a large docking and refuelling complex on Luna.


Mars


UAC is the main financer for Mars City. Mars City is home to 6000 people, made up of mostly scientists and researchers. There are 20,000 Robot workers. UAC also owns 6 argent plasma extraction plants and 3 processing plants on Mars.

UAC has 2 observation outposts and 1 scientific research facility on Phobos.

UAC has a military testing facility on Deimos, as well as 2 military research facilities.
