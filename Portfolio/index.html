ABOUT ME

I am a student studying at the College of Creative Arts at Massey University, Wellington, New Zealand. I enjoy all aspects of photography but I particularly like macro photography, making use of a 58mm reverse ring mounted on a 18-55mm EFS Canon Lens. I also enjoy playing computer games with my favourite genres being Strategy and RTS, City Building, and survival.

I use a Canon EOS 500D (also known as the Rebel T1i) with a standard 18-55mm lens.
